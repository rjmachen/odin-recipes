Description:

Succulent shrimp sautéed in garlic-infused butter and olive oil, finished with fresh lemon juice, white wine, and herbs for a bright, savory classic.

How to Make Shrimp Scampi

  1. Season peeled and deveined shrimp lightly with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté briefly until fragrant.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque on both sides.
  5. Deglaze the pan with white wine and fresh lemon juice.
  6. Simmer briefly, then finish with fresh herbs and additional butter if desired.
  7. Serve immediately over pasta or with crusty bread.
