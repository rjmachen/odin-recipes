Tuna Carpaccio Recipe

Home

Description:

Thinly sliced, sashimi-grade tuna drizzled with citrus and olive oil, delicately garnished for a light, refined, and elegant starter./p>

Ingredients

  • 8 oz sashimi-grade tuna
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 tbsp capers (optional)
  • 1 tbsp fresh herbs or microgreens
  • ½ tsp citrus zest (optional)

How to Make Tuna Carpaccio

  1. Use sashimi-grade tuna, chilled and sliced very thin.
  2. Arrange the tuna slices evenly on a chilled plate.
  3. Drizzle lightly with high-quality olive oil and fresh citrus juice.
  4. Season with salt and freshly cracked black pepper.
  5. Add delicate garnishes such as herbs, capers, or citrus zest.
  6. Serve immediately as a cold appetizer.
